# Branding — Working Document

## Name Candidates

Twenty directions, loosely grouped by vibe.

### Speed / Flow
1. **Warp** — Fast, immediate. Also a weaving term (warp and weft = threads). Short, punchy, verb-able ("warp me the link").
2. **Flux** — Continuous flow, real-time. Modern feel. Implies movement and change.
3. **Relay** — Passing messages forward. Simple, functional, trustworthy. Slightly utilitarian.
4. **Arc** — Clean, minimal. A connection between two points. Also implies narrative arc (conversations have them).

### Structure / Connection
5. **Lattice** — Mathematical structure (Matrix adjacent without being on-the-nose). Interconnected nodes. Premium feel.
6. **Mesh** — Network topology. Simple, technical, evokes decentralization without saying it.
7. **Loom** — Weaving threads together. "Threads" is literally a chat concept. Tactile, craft-oriented.
8. **Nexus** — The connection point. Implies centrality and importance. Bold.

### Place / Gathering
9. **Agora** — Ancient Greek public square. Where people gathered to talk. Intellectual, distinctive.
10. **Cove** — A sheltered inlet. Safe, intimate, private. Good encryption connotation without being techy.
11. **Forum** — Classical gathering place for discourse. Direct, familiar, but maybe too associated with web forums.
12. **Hearth** — The center of the home. Warmth, gathering, core. Slightly cozy for a team tool?

### Material / Texture
13. **Onyx** — Dark, polished, hard. Dark-mode-first energy. Premium, mineral, distinctive.
14. **Flint** — Strikes sparks. Hard, reliable, elemental. Implies igniting conversation.
15. **Prism** — Light refracted into spectrum. Transformation, clarity. Subtle encryption nod (data in, different data out).

### Signal / Communication
16. **Beacon** — A signal that cuts through noise. Guidance, clarity, visibility. Slightly serious.
17. **Cadence** — The rhythm of conversation. Musical, human, implies flow and pacing.
18. **Hail** — A greeting, a call across distance. Short, strong, action-oriented.

### Abstract / Evocative
19. **Kine** — Old English for "kin." Family, tribe, belonging. Short, unusual, ownable.
20. **Volta** — A turn or shift (as in a sonnet's volta). Implies a new direction. Also electric energy (Alessandro Volta).

## Evaluation Criteria

When we narrow down, worth checking each name against:

- **Say it out loud.** Does it sound good? Can you say "send it on [name]" or "check [name]" naturally?
- **Type it.** Is it fast to type? No awkward letter combos?
- **Domain availability.** `.app`, `.chat`, `.io`, or `.com`?
- **App store conflict.** Is the name already taken on iOS/Android/macOS stores?
- **Visual identity.** Can you picture a logo? An icon? Does it suggest a color palette?
- **Global intelligibility.** Does it work across English, European, and Asian language contexts? Any unfortunate meanings?

## Availability Research

### "Agora" — REJECTED

Researched 2026-03-25. **Not viable for a chat application.**

**Dealbreaker: Agora.io (NASDAQ: API)**
- Publicly traded real-time communications company (Santa Clara, CA)
- Sells voice, video, and **chat SDKs** — literally the same product category
- Dominates the namespace: agora.io, AgoraIO on GitHub, @AgoraIO on X, `agora` on npm, `agora_chat_sdk` on pub.dev
- Multiple App Store entries (Agora Conferencing, Agora Live, Agora Flexible Classroom)
- Would face near-certain trademark opposition in Classes 9, 38, 42

**Domains — all taken:**
| Domain | Owner/Status |
|--------|-------------|
| agora.com | The Agora Companies (since 1994) |
| agora.io | Agora, Inc. (NASDAQ: API) |
| agora.app | AFFINITIZ (France) |
| agora.chat | Parked/landing page (AWS) |
| agora.dev | Registered, dormant |
| agora.so | Registered (2023) |
| agorachat.com | **Parked on Afternic (for sale)** |
| getagora.com | Payscale (acquired) — expires May 2026 |
| tryagora.com | Redirects to Agora Data |

**GitHub:**
- `github.com/agora` — taken (Icelandic governance tools org)
- `github.com/AgoraIO` — taken (Agora.io's official org, dominant)
- `github.com/agorachat` — **available**

**App Stores:**
- iOS: Agora.io has multiple apps. An AI meal planner called "agora." also exists.
- Android: An activist social network called "Agora" with encrypted messaging already exists (place.pathos)
- macOS: Agora.io apps + "Agora 2" podcast player

**Social:**
- @agora on X — taken
- @agorachat on X — taken
- @agora on Bluesky — taken
- @agora on Mastodon — taken (dormant)

**Other concerns:**
- "Agora" was a notorious dark web marketplace (~2015, defunct) — still dominates some search results
- The word is generic Greek — heavily used across tech, finance, governance, education

**Verdict:** Too crowded, direct competitor owns the namespace, legal risk too high.

**Strengths:** The metaphor is *perfect* for a chat app — people gathering in a circle to make contact, to communicate across distance and barriers.
